## Ingredients

- 10 oz frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
- 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of mushroom soup
- 1/3 cup chipotle sauce (adjust to taste)
- 16 corn tortillas
- 3 cups shredded cheddar jack cheese
- 1 tbsp oil, for pan-frying tortillas

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Lightly grease a baking dish.
2. Warm spinach in a skillet until excess water cooks off, or heat frozen spinach and pat dry. Set aside.
3. Whisk cream of mushroom soup and chipotle sauce together to make the sauce.
4. Pan-fry each tortilla ~15–20 sec per side until warmed and softened.
5. Toss each pan-fried tortilla in the sauce to coat.
6. Fill with spinach cheese mixture, roll, place seam-side down in dish.
7. Pour remaining sauce over top, sprinkle with remaining cheese.
8. Bake 5 min at 325°F, until cheese is melted and bubbly.
