## Ingredients

**Shrimp**
- Frozen shrimp, thawed and patted dry
- Cornstarch (for coating)
- Egg wash (egg, beaten)
- Unsweetened coconut flakes
- Oil for pan-frying

**Sauce**
- 1/2 cup kewpie mayo
- 2 small Thai chilies, minced fine
- 1 tsp rice vinegar
- 1/2 tsp honey (adjust to taste)

## Steps

1. Thaw shrimp in cold water bath, 10–15 min; pat very dry.
2. Set up three shallow bowls in a row: cornstarch, beaten egg, coconut flakes.
3. Dredge shrimp lightly in cornstarch, shake off excess.
4. Dip in egg wash, fully coating; let excess drip off a second before moving on.
5. Press into coconut flakes gently so they stick on all sides, including the ends.
6. Set breaded shrimp on a tray, not touching, until ready to fry.
7. Pan-fry at moderate heat, about 2–3 minutes per side, until golden and crisp — flip gently once (coconut flakes fall off easier than breadcrumbs).
8. For sauce: mix mayo, minced chilies, rice vinegar, and honey in a bowl.
9. Let sauce rest 5 minutes to let flavors meld.
10. Taste and adjust — more honey to mellow, more vinegar for brightness, water to thin if needed.
