## Ingredients

- 2/3 lb ground beef
- Garlic powder (coating the beef, no fresh garlic)
- 2/3 cup Fantini Sangiovese
- 1 jar Rao's marinara sauce
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt to taste
- 1/2 cup whole milk
- 12 oz rigatoni or penne
- 3/4 cup grated parmesan

## Steps

1. Brown ground beef in olive oil, coating with garlic powder as it cooks (no fresh garlic).
2. Deglaze with Sangiovese, scrape up fond, reduce by half.
3. Add Rao's marinara + red pepper flakes, simmer.
4. Lower heat, stir in milk gently, don't boil.
5. Toss with cooked pasta, loosen with pasta water as needed.
6. Off heat, stir in parmesan. Serve.
