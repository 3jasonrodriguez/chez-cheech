## Ingredients

- Oil, for the pan
- Thai Kitchen red curry paste
- Coconut milk
- Soy sauce
- Cod fillets
- Lemon juice

## Steps

1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add curry paste, sauté 1–2 min until fragrant.
2. Stir in coconut milk and soy sauce. Simmer 5–8 min to build flavor.
3. Nestle cod into the sauce. Baste every minute or so until opaque and flakes easily (~6 min).
4. Remove from heat, stir in lemon juice. Taste and adjust.
