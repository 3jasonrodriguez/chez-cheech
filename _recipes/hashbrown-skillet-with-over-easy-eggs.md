## Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded potato, squeezed dry
- 2 tbsp butter, divided
- 1/2 small onion, diced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 eggs

## Steps

1. Heat 1 tbsp butter in a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add onion and cook until soft, about 3 minutes.
3. Add shredded potato in an even layer, season with salt and pepper, and press down. Cook undisturbed 5–6 minutes until deep golden on the bottom.
4. Flip in sections and cook another 4–5 minutes until crisp throughout.
5. Push hashbrowns to one side, add remaining butter, and fry eggs over easy, about 2 minutes per side.
6. Plate together and serve immediately.
