## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breasts/thighs (frozen, thawed in cold water)
- Flour, for dredging
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 3/4 cup marsala wine
- 3/4 cup chicken stock
- Olive oil
- Salt, pepper, fresh thyme (optional)

## Steps

1. Pat thawed chicken completely dry, lightly dredge in flour.
2. Sear in olive oil over medium-high heat, 4–5 min per side, until golden and 165°F internal; remove, set aside.
3. In same pan, sauté mushrooms 5–7 min until browned (add splash more oil if needed).
4. Add garlic, cook 30 sec.
5. Pour in marsala wine, simmer 3–4 min, scraping up browned bits.
6. Add chicken stock, simmer 5 min to reduce and thicken (simmer longer or use a cornstarch slurry for thicker sauce).
7. Return chicken to pan, simmer 2–3 min in sauce to finish.
