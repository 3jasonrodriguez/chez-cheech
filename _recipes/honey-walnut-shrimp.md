## Ingredients

- 1 lb shrimp, peeled & deveined
- 1 cup candied walnuts
- 1/2 cup kewpie mayo
- 3 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp honey greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp water
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- Cornstarch, for coating
- 2 mandarins
- Oil for frying
- Spinach base

## Steps

1. Boil walnuts 1–2 minutes, drain. Add walnuts to syrupy water/sugar mix in a small pan once sugar is turning gold. Leave walnuts to dry on parchment.
2. Coat shrimp in cornstarch, fry until golden.
3. Mix mayo, honey, and yogurt for the sauce.
4. Toss fried shrimp in the sauce (not in the pan).
5. Top with candied walnuts and diced mandarins, serve immediately.
