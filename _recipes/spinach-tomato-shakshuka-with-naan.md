## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 onion, diced
- Garlic (not fresh — garlic powder/jarred)
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Hint of smoked paprika
- Mushroom umami seasoning
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2 cans (28 oz) diced tomatoes
- 12 oz chopped spinach
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 5 large eggs
- Crumbled feta cheese
- Naan bread, warmed

## Steps

1. Soften onion in olive oil.
2. Add garlic, cumin, paprika, smoked paprika, mushroom umami, and red pepper flakes; bloom briefly.
3. Add first can of diced tomatoes, simmer ~7 min, then stir in second can. Simmer to thicken.
4. Stir in chopped spinach, salt, and pepper. Cook until combined.
5. Make wells in the sauce, crack in eggs.
6. Cover and cook until whites are set, yolks still soft.
7. Warm naan alongside.
8. Off heat, top with crumbled feta. Serve with naan for scooping.
